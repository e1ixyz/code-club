>>> The New School Code Club is a non-career-oriented club devoted to approaching code as a creative and social practice. We are taking an anti-capitalist approach to code and aim to explore code as a practice of community building, social justice, and liberation. We plan to be a supportive and collaborative collective with a political and socially conscious lens on programming, incorporating anticapitalism, social justice, and critical digital theory into the ideology of our practice (read: no NFT bros).
